Credit Research & Ratings

Comprehensive Credit Insights for Informed Lending and Investment Decisions

At Prompt Solutions, we provide end-to-end credit research and rating support that enables investors, lenders, and rating agencies to assess creditworthiness with clarity and confidence. From investment-grade corporates to high-yield issuers, our analysis is grounded in deep sector expertise, global market knowledge, and rigorous financial evaluation.

Our Capabilities

Fundamental Credit Assessment

Evaluating financial health, business stability, and market positioning

Credit Memos & Rating Rationales

Preparing high-quality internal and external credit documentation

Industry & Peer Benchmarking

Comparing key financial and operational metrics across competitors

Solvency & Leverage Analysis

Examining liquidity, capital structure, and debt-servicing ability

Event Impact Studies

Assessing credit implications of M&A, restructuring, refinancing, or downgrades

Press Releases & Reporting

Drafting rating actions, investor updates, and analytical summaries

Our Approach

Holistic Risk Analysis

Integrating financial, operational, regulatory, and macroeconomic factors

Data-Driven Evaluation

Using Bloomberg, Capital IQ, and FactSet for accurate credit data

Scenario Testing

Modeling various economic and operational stress scenarios.

Tailored Output

Reports aligned with each client's methodology and rating scale
